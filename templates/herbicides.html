Selective vs Non-Selective

Selective herbicides kill specific weeds without harming your crop. Non-selective (Glyphosate/Muddosate) clears all vegetation — ideal for land preparation before planting.

When to Apply

Apply post-emergence when weeds are young (2–4 leaf stage) for best results. Pre-harvest intervals must be observed. Call us for crop-specific timing advice.
