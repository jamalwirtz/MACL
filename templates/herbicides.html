Selective vs Non-Selective

Selective herbicides kill specific weeds without harming your crop. Non-selective (like Muddosate/Glyphosate) kill all vegetation — ideal for land clearing. Our team will help you choose.

Timing Matters

Apply post-emergence herbicides when weeds are young (2–4 leaf stage) for best results. Pre-harvest intervals must be observed. Call us for crop-specific timing advice.
