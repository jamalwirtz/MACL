Rotate Your Fungicides

Don't use the same fungicide every season — rotate between contact and systemic types to prevent resistance build-up. Our team can design a rotation program for your farm.

Preventive vs Curative

Preventive fungicides protect healthy tissue. Curative fungicides like SCORE can stop an active infection. Apply before or at the very start of disease symptoms for best results.

Safe for Organic Transition

Copper-based fungicides like COPPER OXYCHLORIDE are suitable for farmers transitioning to organic production. Check with our agronomists for certification requirements.
