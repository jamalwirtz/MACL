Rotate Fungicides Every Season

Using the same fungicide repeatedly leads to resistance. Rotate between contact and systemic types each season. Our team can design a rotation program for your farm.

Preventive vs Curative

Preventive fungicides protect healthy tissue before infection. Curative types (TOPLAXYL-M, SCORE) stop active infections. Apply at first signs of disease for best curative results.

Safe Application

Always wear protective equipment when applying fungicides. Observe pre-harvest intervals. Store away from food and children. Dispose of empty containers responsibly per NEMA guidelines.
