About MACL

Muddo Agro Chemicals Ltd is a registered distributor of agricultural inputs, headquartered in Kampala's Container Village Nakivubo.

Our Reach

Operating across all four regions of Uganda — Central, Eastern, Northern and Western — with 10+ authorised outlet stores.

Our Mission

To make quality, affordable agrochemicals accessible to every Ugandan farmer, improving yields and food security nationwide.

Pesticides & Insecticides

Broad-spectrum and selective solutions — Muddosate, MD FOS, MD Acelemectin, Top Fenos and more.

Herbicides

Selective and non-selective weed control — MD-Maize Plus, MAX 2.4D, MD Ametryn, Weed IT 75.7 XL, Toplaxly.

Fertilizers & Equipment

Urea, NPK blends, foliar feeds, and quality knapsack sprayers for complete crop nutrition and protection.

MAAIF Registered

All products are registered with Uganda's Ministry of Agriculture, ensuring full regulatory compliance and authentic quality.

100% Authentic

Sourced directly from manufacturers with full documentation. Zero counterfeit products — ever.

Expert Support

Our field agents provide agronomic advice, dosage guidance, and crop-specific recommendations to every customer.
