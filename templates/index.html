About Us

Muddo Agro Chemicals LTD

Based in Kampala's Container Village Nakivubo, Muddo Agro Chemicals LTD is a registered distributor and retailer of high-quality agrochemicals serving farmers across Uganda.

We are distributors of both pesticides and insecticides — non-selective and selective — providing affordable, government-approved solutions that help farmers combat weeds, pests, and diseases effectively.
