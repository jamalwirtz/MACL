Who We Are

MACL — Muddo Agro Chemicals LTD

Based in Kampala's Container Village Nakivubo, Equity Bank Basement, Muddo Agro Chemicals LTD is a Ministry of Agriculture (MAAIF)-registered distributor of pesticides, herbicides, fungicides, fertilizers, and spraying equipment serving farmers across all regions of Uganda.

We partner directly with world-class manufacturers in India, China, and Europe to bring you authentic, proven agrochemical solutions at competitive prices — with no counterfeit products, ever.
